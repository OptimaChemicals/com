PRODUCTION CHEMICALS

To enhance well flow, Optima Chemicals provides a comprehensive range of standard production chemicals, including wellbore cleaners, scale inhibitors, foamers, dissolvers, paraffin and asphaltene solvents, H2S scavengers, and biocides. We also offer customizable demulsifiers tailored to specific site requirements. Most importantly, Optima Chemicals delivers exceptional laboratory support, offering precise and innovative solutions to optimize your well production. Our team is ready to collaborate with you to develop the precise chemical solutions needed to achieve your production objectives.

 

The current product line includes:

i. Emulsion Breakers:separate the produced water from the oil

ii. Paraffin and Asphaltene Solvents: dissolve asphaltenes, wax, and hydrocarbon residues

iii. Scale Dissolvers: effectively remove organic deposits from all equipment parts

iv. Scale Inhibitors: act as a preventative solution for controlling inorganic deposits

v. Biocides: keep bacteria growth under control

vi. H2S Scavengers: reduce levels of toxic (H2S) gas

vii. Foaming Agents: enhance oil recovery

viii. Defoamers: mitigate foam problems
